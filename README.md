# 5-Year-Financial-Forecast-Valuation-Model
**Project:** Corporate Financial Forecasting & Performance Analysis

## The Objective
The goal was to create a dynamic model that translates strategic assumptions—such as revenue growth and cost efficiencies—into a complete financial picture

---

## Core Assumptions
The model is built on a set of conservative yet growth-oriented assumptions for the period of 2024–2028:

###  Revenue & Growth
* **2024 Growth:** 18.0%
* **2025 Growth:** 16.0%
* **2026 Growth:** 14.0%
* **2027 Growth:** 12.0%
* **2028 Growth:** 10.0%

###  Profitability & Tax
* **COGS (% of Revenue):** Starts at 64.5% in 2024, decreasing by **0.5% each year** to simulate operational efficiency.
* **SG&A (% of Revenue):** Kept flat at 19.0%.
* **Tax Rate:** 21.0%.

###  Working Capital
* **DSO (Receivables):** 40 Days.
* **DIH (Inventory):** 50 Days.
* **DPO (Payables):** 35 Days.

###  Investing & Financing
* **CapEx:** $12,000 in 2024, growing by 4% annually.
* **Depreciation:** 10% of the Beginning PP&E balance.
* **Debt Repayment:** $1,500 annual reduction of loan principal.
* **Dividends:** 5% of Net Income (starting in 2024).

---

## 3. Financial Health Analysis

####  Is the company generating enough cash to stay healthy?**
 The model shows a very strong cash trajectory. The ending cash balance grows from **$18,000 in 2023 to over $70,000 by 2028**

---

## 4. Technical Features of the Model

###  Fully Integrated Circularity
The "Ending Cash" from the Cash Flow Statement automatically updates the "Cash" line on the Balance Sheet. This integration ensures the model always stays in balance and reflects the true liquidity of the firm.

###  Automated Schedules
The model includes built-in **Depreciation and CapEx schedules** that automatically adjust based on the aging of assets and planned new investments.

###  Dynamic Assumptions Table
A centralized dashboard serves as the control center for the model. Users can modify a single growth rate or margin percentage and witness the entire 5-year forecast update instantly across all three statements.
